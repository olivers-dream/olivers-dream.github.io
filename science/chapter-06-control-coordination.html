🗺 Mind Map: Key Concepts

Central concept connects to main topics.

Core Concepts

  • Nervous system: CNS (brain, spinal cord), PNS (nerves).
  • Reflex: automatic response without brain thinking.
  • Hormones: chemical messengers for long-term coordination.
  • Plant hormones: auxins, gibberellins control growth.

Formulas & Equations

  • Reflex arc: stimulus → receptor → neuron → effector → response

Worked Examples

Example 1: Reflex involves brain?

Expected answer: No

Solution: Reflex is automatic, spinal cord response only

Example 2: Example of plant hormone?

Expected answer: Auxin or Gibberellin

Solution: Control growth direction and rate

Practice Questions

EASY Q1: Brain part: thinking and memory?
Answer: Cerebrum
EASY Q2: Spinal cord part of?
Answer: CNS (central nervous system)
EASY Q3: Pupil reflex needs thinking?
Answer: No, automatic
MED Q4: Neurotransmitter role?
Answer: Carry signals between neurons
MED Q5: Hormone insulin does?
Answer: Lower blood glucose
MED Q6: Auxin effect on plant?
Answer: Promotes cell elongation
HARD Q7: Adrenaline effect?
Answer: Fight-or-flight: heart rate up, pupils dilate
HARD Q8: Difference: impulse vs hormone?
Answer: Impulse fast electrical, hormone slow chemical
HARD Q9: Gibberellin and germination?
Answer: Gibberellin promotes germination
